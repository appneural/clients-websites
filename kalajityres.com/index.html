Customers FAQ’s

I have a flat tire, can KalajiTyres help me?

Yes! We offer a wide variety of tire and tube repair services. We also have a variety of products to help you with your flat tire. We have a product called the KalajiTyres Tire Plugger that is a quick and easy way to fix your tire.

What is the best way to repair a tire?

There is no one definitive answer to this question as it depends on the specific tire, the extent of the damage, and other factors. However, in general, the best way to repair a tire is to patch it from the inside. This will ensure that the patch holds and that the tire does not lose air pressure.

I have a tire that needs repair, can I bring it to KalajiTyres?

Yes, you can bring your tire to KalajiTyres for a repair. We would be happy to take a look at it and give you an estimate.
