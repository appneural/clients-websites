KalajiTyres is a leading provider of OTR tyres and services. We have over 30 years of experience in the industry and offer a wide range of products and services to meet the needs of our customers. We also offer a wide range of services such as tyre tracking and record keeping, to help reduce operational costs and improve tyre life.
